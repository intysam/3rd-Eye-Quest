How to Play

1. The Judge Selects a Target

One player acts as the Judge and secretly draws a card from the Landscape, Animal, Color, or Object decks. They keep the image hidden from the other players.

2. Viewers Sense the Unseen

Viewers close their eyes, breathe deeply, and record their intuitive impressions. Use the Digital Assist Guide to probe for textures, emotions, and shapes.

3. Reveal and Reward

The Judge reveals the card. The player whose description most accurately matches the target wins the round and keeps the card. The most intuitive player is crowned the Master Viewer!
