How to Play

3rd Eye Quest is a fast, intuitive remote viewing game designed for groups, classrooms, and curious explorers.

  1. Select a Judge. The Judge secretly draws a target card and keeps it hidden.
  2. Viewers describe impressions. Players write textures, shapes, colors, emotions, movement, and sensory clues.
  3. Reveal the target. The Judge compares the sessions to the card image.
  4. Choose the closest match. The winning Viewer keeps the card.

The golden rule: describe, don’t define. Let the first signal speak before the mind starts decorating it.
