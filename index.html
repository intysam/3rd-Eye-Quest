What It Is

3rd Eye Quest turns real remote viewing principles into a fun, repeatable game night format. No pressure. No “psychic performance.” Just structured rounds that train perception.

What you do

Close eyes, relax, and write quick impressions: texture, shape, emotion, movement, temperature.

What you’re training

First-signal accuracy, sensory vocabulary, and discipline — the building blocks of remote viewing.

Why it’s addictive

Instant feedback. Friendly competition. And you get better the more you play.

How to Play

Three steps. Fast rounds. Big “wait… how did you know that?” moments.

1. The Judge selects a target

One player secretly draws a card (Landscape, Animal, Color, or Object) and keeps the image hidden. Everyone else becomes a Viewer.

2. Viewers sense the unseen

Viewers close their eyes, breathe, and record impressions — describe instead of guessing. Use the Digital Assist Guide to probe for texture, shape, mood, and movement.

3. Reveal + reward

The Judge reveals the card. The closest description wins the round and keeps the card. The most consistent player becomes the Master Viewer.

"Describe, don’t define. Trust your first signal."
