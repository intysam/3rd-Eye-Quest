How to Play

1. The Judge Selects a Target

One player acts as the Judge and secretly draws a card from the Landscape, Animal, Color, or Object decks. They keep the image hidden from others.

2. Viewers Sense the Unseen

Viewers close their eyes and describe intuitive impressions: textures, shapes, and emotions. Remember the golden rule: Describe, don't define.

3. Reveal and Reward

The Judge reveals the card. The player whose description most accurately matches the target wins the round and keeps the card.
