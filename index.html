How to Play

1. The Judge Selects a Target

One player acts as the Judge and secretly draws a card from the Landscape, Animal, Color, or Object decks[cite: 244, 248, 249]. They keep the image hidden from the other players[cite: 250].

2. Viewers Sense the Unseen

Viewers close their eyes, breathe deeply, and record their intuitive impressions[cite: 251, 254, 255]. Use the Assist Cards or our Digital Assist Guide to probe for textures, emotions, and shapes[cite: 245, 256].

3. Reveal and Reward

The Judge reveals the card[cite: 260]. The player whose description most accurately matches the target wins the round and keeps the card[cite: 241, 261]. The most intuitive player is crowned the Master Viewer[cite: 235, 242]!
