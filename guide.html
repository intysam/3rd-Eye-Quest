Digital Assist Guide

When it is your turn to probe, remember:

  • Choose one player to target.
  • Ask a question based on your current Quest card.
  • Watch for physical cues or hesitations.
  • Visual Cues: Eye contact shifts or blinking patterns.
  • Verbal Cues: Changes in pitch or "filler" words (um, ah).
  • Physical: Touching the face or adjusting clothes.

Look for micro-expressions that contradict their words:

  • Quick scoffs or lip biting.
  • Tension in the jaw or shoulders.
