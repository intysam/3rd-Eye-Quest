A Quick Reference for Intuitive Probing
The Golden Rules:
• Relax your mind before drawing impressions.
• Describe, don’t define.
• Trust your first signal.

Landscape & Environment

  • Terrain: Flat, rocky, uneven, or elevated?
  • Origin: Does it feel natural, man-made, or a blend?
  • Vibe: Urban, rural, or harsh natural?
  • Vegetation: Lush, sparse, or barren?
  • Mood: Peaceful, oppressive, or awe-inspiring?

Animal Energy

  • Nature: Calm, fierce, cautious, or playful?
  • Physical: Size (small to massive) and Texture (fur, scales, feathers)?
  • Movement: Crawling, flying, swimming, or still?
  • Connection: Do you feel kinship or caution toward it?

Object & Artifacts

  • Energy: Still, active, mechanical, or sacred?
  • Physicality: Heavy or light? Solid or flexible?
  • Material: Metal, stone, wood, fabric, or crystal?
  • Surface: Shiny, matte, translucent, or opaque?

Color & Aura

  • Primary Hue: What are the main shades you perceive?
  • Tone: Bright, muted, pastel, or deep?
  • Element: Does it belong to sky, earth, water, fire, or aura?
