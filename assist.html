"Describe, don’t define. Trust your first signal."

Landscape & Environment

  • Terrain: Is it flat, rocky, uneven, or elevated?
  • Vegetation: Does it feel lush, sparse, or barren?
  • Water: Is it still, flowing, or frozen?
  • Light: Is it bright, dim, or glowing?

Sensory Signals

  • Temperature: Does it feel warm, cold, or shifting?
  • Texture: Is it smooth, rough, metallic, or soft?
  • Energy: Does it feel still, active, or chaotic?
  • Mood: Is it peaceful, mysterious, or awe-inspiring?

Animal & Life

  • Energy: Is it calm, fierce, cautious, or playful?
  • Size: Small, medium, or massive?
  • Texture: Fur, feathers, scales, or smooth skin?
  • Movement: Crawling, flying, swimming, or still?
