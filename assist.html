Landscape

  • Terrain Flat, rocky, uneven, or elevated?
  • Environment Urban, rural, natural, or harsh?
  • Vegetation Lush, sparse, or barren?
  • Water Still, flowing, or frozen?
  • Mood Reverent, peaceful, or oppressive?

Animal

  • Energy Calm, fierce, cautious, or playful?
  • Size Small, medium, large, or massive?
  • Texture Fur, feathers, scales, or smooth?
  • Movement Crawling, flying, swimming, or still?
  • Location Air, land, water, or sky?

Object

  • Nature Functional, artistic, or symbolic?
  • Weight Does it feel heavy or light?
  • Material Metal, stone, wood, fabric, or crystal?
  • Reflectivity Shiny, matte, or translucent?
  • Density Is it solid or flexible?

Color & Energy

  • Tone Bright, muted, pastel, or deep?
  • Temp Warm (reds/golds) or Cool (blues/greens)?
  • Emotion Does it feel warm, cool, or calm?
  • Origin Sky, earth, water, fire, or aura?
